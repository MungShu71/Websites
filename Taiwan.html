Top 5 Food in Taiwan

  2. Dumplings

    Dough with filling stuffed inside. Makes you look like Po from Kung Fu Panda.

  3. Friuts

    Fruits actually from plants and not from plastic cups bathing in its own juices. The varity of fruits in Taiwan is extensive, but I prefer dem thicc mangos.

  4. Ramen

    Imperative for college life. Ramen made in Taiwan are safe and won't kill you within a month.
